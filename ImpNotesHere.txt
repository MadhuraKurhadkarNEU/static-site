Add Credentials in Jenkins (Everytime when resources are created):

Open Jenkins and navigate to Manage Jenkins > Manage Credentials.
Add new credentials:
Kind: Username with password.
Username: Your DockerHub username.
Password: The token generated.
ID: Set this to dockerhub-credentials-id.
